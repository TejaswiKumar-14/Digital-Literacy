# Digital-Literacy

##  Overview

This project focuses on **Digital Literacy**, which means understanding how to use digital tools, communicate effectively online, and follow safe and responsible practices while using the internet.

The project includes practical tasks such as email writing, social media awareness, and collaboration using Google Workspace tools.

---

##  Objectives

* To understand the importance of digital communication
* To learn professional email etiquette
* To promote responsible social media usage
* To explore online collaboration tools
* To improve overall digital skills for academic and professional use

### Task 4: Email Etiquette & Social Media

* Professional email examples
* Social media Do’s and Don’ts checklist
* Awareness of digital communication mistakes


##  Social Media Checklist

###  Do’s

* Be respectful and polite
* Verify information before sharing
* Use strong privacy settings
* Think before posting
* Maintain a positive digital footprint

### Don’ts

* Don’t share fake news
* Avoid posting personal information publicly
* Don’t engage in online arguments
* Avoid offensive or harmful content
* Don’t misuse social platforms



##  Importance of Digital Literacy

Digital literacy helps individuals:

* Communicate effectively online
* Stay safe from cyber threats
* Use digital tools efficiently
* Build a professional online presence
